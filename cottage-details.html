Cottages

Single Cottage

Perfect for solo travelers seeking comfort and privacy. The room features a comfortable single-size bed, a workspace, and all the necessary amenities to ensure a comfortable stay. It is ideal for those on a budget, without compromising comfort.

Available with the All-inclusive Plan.
